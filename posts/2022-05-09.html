Experiments in Education

I came back from Black Mountain yesterday. I got dropped off by two other students in Durham and then took the Amtrak to Charlotte.

It was a profoundly magical week.

Today I didn’t make any art. My big project since I came back has been organizing my room, building some furniture, cleaning my closet. I feel transformed but I don’t know how to convert emotion in to action. I think this was the change I was looking for but it’ll be hard to know until I move forward with this change.
